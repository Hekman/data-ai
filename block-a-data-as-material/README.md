# Block A — Data as Design Material

**Block question:** *What does this data actually let me know, and what stories can I tell?*
Treats data as a designer's material — something you source, question, shape and compose with.
Targets **PLO 5** (Data Science Workflow & Analytical Reasoning) at *Advanced Beginner*.

## Project

Block A builds toward a **data-informed story for a real audience**. Students shortlist datasets
from Week 1 and commit to one by Week 3.

> order above. Each week has the matching solution notebook in `solutions/` (gitignored). Week 1
> has a full Slidev deck; Weeks 2–7 have starter decks (`slides.md`) to build out.
